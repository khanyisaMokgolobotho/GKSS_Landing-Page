About the day

Ga-Rankuwa Career Tech Expo presents a learner-first career experience.

The event is designed to help high school learners, especially Grade 12 learners, explore technology careers, understand how AI is reshaping work, and make more confident decisions about life after school.

01

Understand AI-era careers

Learners get a grounded introduction to roles in AI, data, software, cybersecurity, and digital business.

02

Meet people already in the field

Speakers and partners share what tech work actually looks like, how they got started, and what skills matter now.

03

Plan the next step after Grade 12

Learners leave with clearer ideas around study options, bursaries, work readiness, and career direction.

Experience pillars

The expo is built around career insight, live demos, and future planning.

Each pillar gives learners a different way to engage, whether they want to listen, ask questions, explore, or plan ahead.

AI Career Spotlights
