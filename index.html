About the expo

A learner-first event built to make tech pathways clearer in the AI era.

The expo is designed to help high school learners explore modern career options, understand what studying into tech can look like, and get closer to the people and organisations shaping those opportunities. Teachers and school partners are also part of the day because learner decisions are stronger when the support around them is informed too.

01

Understand AI-era careers

Break down how AI is reshaping roles across software, data, cybersecurity, design, and digital operations.

02

Connect learning to real pathways

See how school subjects, post-school study routes, and practical skills connect to technology careers.

03

Hear from people in the field

Panel conversations and showcases make the journey into tech feel more practical, local, and reachable.

04

Support better next-step decisions

Learners, teachers, and partner organisations all leave with clearer signals on what comes after school.
